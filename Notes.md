**Features:**



Log meals -

time and date, meal, insulin dosage, sugar level before meal, amount of time since injection, sugar level 2 h. after meal, free note, Insert into category, set reminder



Log work-outs -

time and date, duration (reminder), sugar level before, food, temp insulin, type, sugar level after work-out, free note, set reminder



Log time periods -

Period (day+reminders), temp basal (custom time+reminders), other (custom time)





Meal list -

Add products with carb contents, search meals, look at stats for meal



Work-out list -

Add work-out, search work-out, look at stats for work-out



Categories -

Make new category and look at meals and work-outs under this category.



Reminders -

Set custom reminders.

Reminder types: check glucose (insert glucose), log meal, log work-out, still valid?



**Plan**



1. meal list
2. calendar
3. general note
4. meal log





**entities:**



food: name, carbs

meal: food, time
